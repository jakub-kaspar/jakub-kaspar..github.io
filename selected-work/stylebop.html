Stylebop

This project centered around developing a native mobile app known as Stylebop, facilitating the online purchase of luxury apparel.
‍
The responsibility involved delivering design assets—comprising screens, prototypes, and specifications—founded on user research.

The challenge was to launch a mobile app for Android and iOS in just 8 months. The app had 10,000+ downloads on Android and 100,000+ downloads on iOS with an average conversion rate of 4%.
