Peek & Cloppenburg

This project involved revamping the primary Peek & Cloppenburg online store, accessible across multiple countries.

The responsibility involved delivering design assets—comprising screens, prototypes, and specifications—founded on comprehensive user research.

The work was conducted within an enthusiastic UX team of approximately 10 individuals, supporting a larger Product team comprising around 150 members, including product owners, developers, and stakeholders.
